History

Yahay Tambayan Boljoon was established on December 3, 2023. It was created as a community hangout where people can relax, meet friends, and enjoy the local atmosphere. Located in the historic town of Boljoon, it blends cultural heritage with a modern and welcoming space for both locals and visitors.

Life of the Founder

The owner of Yahay Tambayan is a single mother who worked in the hotel and restaurant industry before starting the business. She is the third among six siblings and graduated from USPF with a degree in Hotel and Restaurant Management. Her experience in various hotels and restaurants helped her gain the skills needed to start her own business.

Success of the Business

Despite financial challenges and personal responsibilities, the owner remained determined and passionate. Through hard work and perseverance, Yahay Tambayan grew into a well-loved hangout spot in the community. Her journey shows that resilience and commitment can turn small beginnings into success.

Challenges

Balancing motherhood and business was not easy. The owner faced financial limitations, long working hours, and competition from other establishments. However, her dedication and strong vision helped her overcome these obstacles.

Future Vision

Ten years from now, the business aims to expand into multiple branches and provide more job opportunities to people in need.

Government Support

The Philippine government helped through programs supporting small entrepreneurs. Through the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI), the owner was able to legally register the business, strengthening credibility and customer trust.
